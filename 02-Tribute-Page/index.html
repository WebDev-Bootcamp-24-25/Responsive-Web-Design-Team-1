Michael Jackson

A Musical Icon Forever Remembered

Here's a timeline of Michael Jackson's life:

  • 1958–1968

    Early Life and The Jackson 5

    • 1958 - Born on August 29 in Gary, Indiana.
    • 1964 - Joins “The Jackson 5”.
    • 1969 - Releases first hit, "I Want You Back".
  • 1971–1982

    Solo Career Begins

    • 1971 - Releases "Got to Be There".
    • 1979 - "Off the Wall" album.
    • 1982 - Releases “Thriller”.
  • 1983–1987

    Global Superstardom

    • 1983 - Debuts the Moonwalk during the Motown 25 performance of "Billie Jean."
    • 1984 - Wins 8 Grammy Awards for “Thriller.”
    • 1987 - Releases "Bad", featuring five No. 1 singles.
  • 1991–1997

    Continued Success

    • 1991 - Releases "Dangerous", featuring "Black or White".
    • 1993 - Performs at the Super Bowl XXVII halftime show.
    • 1995 - Releases "HIStory", a double album.
  • 2001–2009

    Later Years

    • 2001 - Releases "Invincible", his final studio album.
    • 2009 - Announced “This Is It” concert series but passed away on June 25.
  • Legacy

    Posthumous Recognition

    • 2009 - "This Is It" documentary released.
    • Today - Michael Jackson’s influence continues.
